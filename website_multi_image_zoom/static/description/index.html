Product Multiple Image Zoom

A good zoom feature for your product with multiple images

This module was written to add the functionality of adding multiple images for product and zoom in/out feature on product image. The Module provides a good zoom feature for your product images, is always a plus point online. As it allows visitors to see multiple images for a product and the minor details of your products, as sometimes customers prefer to feel and touch, so you can easily replace this by having a good zoom feature with multiple image which really brings out the details in your product.
